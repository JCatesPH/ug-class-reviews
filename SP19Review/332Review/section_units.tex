\textbf{Useful conversions:}

\[
\frac{N}{Cm} = \frac{W}{m^2s} \implies \frac{W}{s} = \frac{Nm}{C} = \frac{J}{C} = V
\]
\[
[E] = \frac{W}{sm} \text{  or  } [E] = \frac{V}{m}
\]
And
\[
[H] = \frac{N}{W} \text{  or  } [H] = \frac{A}{m}
\]

\textbf{\textcolor{red}{Memorize:}}
\[
J = A \cdot W
